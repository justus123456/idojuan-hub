Add New Property

Select one or more images (JPG/PNG). They will be uploaded and stored.
How this works
  • Fill in the form above to add a new property (title, type, location, price, details).
  • You can upload multiple images — they will be saved and displayed in the property details page.
  • Interior and exterior amenities help buyers see what’s included (e.g. Air Conditioning, Garden, Parking).
  • When you click Save Property, the information is stored in your Supabase database — not just your browser.
  • The listings will then appear live on your website’s property page and detail pages.
  • Use the Preview button to quickly check how the property will look for clients.
  • Export/Import lets you back up and restore data if needed.
